How it works?

Upon the selection of a command by an approved user, the complete message will be transmitted to the drone base, where it will undergo execution. Examples of such commands may include tasks such as opening doors or inspecting the drone's battery level. It is noteworthy that the entire hangar is sustainably powered by solar energy, ensuring uninterrupted operations throughout both day and night.

Requirements for the Drone Hangar:

Additionally, finding a suitable location is essential; it must accommodate several drones and be well-lit to harness energy from solar panels. Another critical aspect is the server itself, which requires a microcomputer for optimal functionality.

To construct such a hangar, various materials are necessary, including:

  • Concrete
  • Carbon polymers
  • Steel beams
  • Gears
  • Thermoplastic carbon fiber
  • solar panels
